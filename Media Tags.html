About Awesmos

The sisters used Hindi poetry to pitch their products, silver jewellery embellished with inspirational messages, rustic designs and calligraphy. Anupam Mittal was unsure if the sisters would be able to scale the products from occasion wear to daily wear, and was also concerned about the lack of growth that the company had witnessed since its inception in 2022. In addition to all these question marks, the sisters were also asking for Rs 80 lakh in exchange of 1% equity, valuing the company at Rs 80 crore.
